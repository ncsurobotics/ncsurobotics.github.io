On September 9th, Aquapack was excited to invite ADI back for a second year. ADI and alumni helped Aquapack members tweak and improve their resumes. Among the ADI panel were James Stanley, Senior Applications Manager at ADI, Meaghan Keane, Team Lead at ADI, and Sara Idris, Mixed Signal Test Engineer at ADI. The panel also had 3 Aquapack alumni: Alex Pendergast, Layout designer at ADI, Daniel Mitchum, Electronics Engineer at ATI, and Amr Moussa, Software Engineer at Relay.

The session did not just focus on resumes but also on internships, career advice, interview prep, and co-op opportunities. A wide range of undergraduates, all the way from new freshmen learning to navigate the professional world to seniors looking for full-time jobs, benefited from this workshop.
