The mechanical subteam has worked very hard to improve the camera from last year and get a better version onto SWIX. Alexandria Epley machined it from 6061 aluminum on a 3-axis Mass vf2 mill.  


{% set alt = "picture of mechanical team lead" %}
{% image "/assets/images/members/Alexandria-Epley.jpg", alt, alt %}

The largest improvement from the old camera to the new camera is the use of an o-ring slotted into a racetrack groove instead of liquid gasket. This makes opening and closing the hull much easier. Dria says "While the club has used o-ring's in the past, this is, to my knowledge, the first member-designed racetrack groove the club has done"
